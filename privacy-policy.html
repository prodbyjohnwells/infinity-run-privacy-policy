Privacy Policy for Infinity Run

Last updated: May 5, 2025

Infinity Run is a mobile arcade game developed by prodbyjohnwells.

1. Information We Collect

While Infinity Run itself does not collect personally identifiable information (PII), the game integrates several third-party services that may collect certain data automatically as part of their functionality:

  • Google Play Games Services: used for leaderboards and achievements. This may access your Google Play Games profile (including display name and ID).
  • Google Sign-In: used for authentication. This may collect your name, email, and profile image if you choose to sign in.
  • Firebase Authentication: supports user login (via Google Sign-In).
  • Firebase Realtime Database: stores and syncs non-PII gameplay data such as scores and progress.
  • Firebase Analytics: collects anonymous app usage data (device type, OS version, in-game events).
  • Firebase Performance Monitoring: tracks app performance and device characteristics (e.g., network speed, app startup time).
  • Firebase Crashlytics: collects crash logs and diagnostic information to improve app stability.
  • Google AdMob: displays ads that may be personalized or contextual. AdMob may collect device information (e.g., IP address, advertising ID, interactions).

All data collected by these services is governed by their own privacy policies. Learn more here: Google Privacy Policy

2. How We Use This Information

Information collected by third-party services is used for the following purposes:

  • To provide leaderboard and account-based features (Google Play Games, Firebase Auth)
  • To show ads and generate revenue (AdMob)
  • To monitor performance and improve game stability (Crashlytics, Performance Monitoring)
  • To understand general app usage patterns (Firebase Analytics)

3. Children's Privacy

Infinity Run is intended for general audiences and is rated “Everyone” on the Google Play Store. While the game does not contain content inappropriate for children, it does not knowingly collect personal data from users under the age of 13. All third-party services used in the app (e.g., Google Play Games, Firebase, AdMob) are configured to restrict data collection and comply with COPPA when applicable.

4. Data Security

All data handled by third-party services is transmitted over secure connections (HTTPS) and stored according to their respective data security practices.
