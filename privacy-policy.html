Privacy Policy for Infinity Run

Last updated: May 5, 2025

Infinity Run is a mobile arcade game developed by prodbyjohnwells.

1. Information We Collect

The game itself does not collect any personally identifiable information (PII). However, it uses Google services including:

  • Google Play Games Services (leaderboards)
  • Google AdMob (ads)
  • Google Analytics for Firebase (usage tracking)
  • Firebase Crashlytics (bug/crash reports)

These services may collect non-personal data such as device type, OS version, and app usage. Learn more: Google Privacy Policy

2. Children's Privacy

The game is rated “Everyone” and does not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. All third-party data is anonymized and follows Google’s child safety policies.

Questions? Contact us at: prodbyjohnwells@gmail.com

This privacy policy may be updated occasionally. Check back for changes.
