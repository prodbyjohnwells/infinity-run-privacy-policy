Privacy Policy for Infinity Run

Last updated: June 12, 2025

Infinity Run is a mobile arcade game developed by prodbyjohnwells.

1. Information We Collect

While Infinity Run itself does not collect personally identifiable information (PII), the game integrates several third-party services that may collect certain data automatically as part of their functionality:

  • Google Play Games Services: used for leaderboards and achievements. This may access your Google Play Games profile (including display name and ID).
  • Google Sign-In: used for authentication. This may collect your name, email, and profile image if you choose to sign in.
  • Firebase Authentication: supports user login (via Google Sign-In).
  • Firebase Realtime Database: stores and syncs non-PII gameplay data such as scores and progress.
  • Firebase Analytics: collects anonymous app usage data (device type, OS version, in-game events).
  • Firebase Performance Monitoring: tracks app performance and device characteristics (e.g., network speed, app startup time).
  • Firebase Crashlytics: collects crash logs and diagnostic information to improve app stability.
  • Google AdMob: displays ads that may be personalized or contextual. AdMob may collect device information (e.g., IP address, advertising ID, interactions).

All data collected by these services is governed by their own privacy policies. Learn more here: Google Privacy Policy

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or United Kingdom, we comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). You will be prompted to provide explicit consent for the use of personal data such as personalized ads or analytics. You may withdraw your consent at any time through your device settings or by contacting us. You can also manage or revoke your consent at any time through the “Privacy Settings” button located in the app’s Settings menu. This allows users in GDPR regions to easily update their preferences regarding personalized ads and data usage.

2. How We Use This Information

The information collected by integrated third-party services is used solely to provide, improve, and maintain a high-quality user experience. Specifically:

  • Google Play Games / Firebase Authentication: Enables leaderboard, achievement, and sign-in functionality.
  • AdMob: Serves ads to support the app financially. Ads may be personalized if you grant consent under GDPR.
  • Firebase Crashlytics & Performance Monitoring: Helps detect crashes, bugs, and performance issues to enhance app stability.
  • Firebase Analytics: Offers insight into anonymous user behavior to inform design and gameplay improvements.

3. Children's Privacy

Infinity Run is designed for general audiences and is rated “Everyone” on the Google Play Store. The app does not knowingly collect or request personal information from children under the age of 13.

All third-party services (including Google Play Games, Firebase, and AdMob) are configured to follow appropriate age-related policies. Where applicable, they restrict data collection in compliance with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

If you believe that your child has provided personal information without your consent, please contact us so we can take steps to remove such data.

4. Data Security

All data handled by third-party services is transmitted over secure connections (HTTPS) and stored according to their respective data security practices.
